Lemon Bars

back to recipe list

Description

Indulge in the enchanting flavors of these bright and zingy lemon bars, where a delicate crisp shortbread crust serves as the perfect canvas for a burst of citrus delight. With a harmonious balance between tangy and sweet, these bars are an effortless yet irresistible crowd-pleaser. One of the many reasons to love these lemon bars is their versatility. Whether enjoyed chilled straight from the fridge or at room temperature, they never fail to captivate with their lusciousness.

Despite the impressive taste, the recipe itself is wonderfully simple, requiring only a handful of ingredients to achieve such a vibrant pairing. Brace yourself for a burst of citrusy bliss as you savor these sensational lemon bars, a truly delectable indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cups powdered sugar (more for dusting)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 12 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 tbsp lemon zest (zest of three lemons)
  • 3 cups granulated sugar
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 cup lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F and line a 9x13 baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together 2 1/2 cups of flour with powdered sugar and salt, then pour in melted butter and mix until combined.
  3. Place dough in lined pan and press down into flat, even layer. Bake at 350F for about 20 minutes or until light golden colour.
  4. Add sugar and lemon zest to food processor. Pulse until zest is fully incorporated and sugar is a light yellow colour.
  5. Add sugar and remaining half cup of flour to a large bowl and mix well.
  6. Pour in lemon juice and eggs, then mix very well until completely combined.
  7. Pour the filling onto the warm crust, then transfer to oven and bake for about 25 minutes. Remove from oven and chill in fridge for two hours.
  8. Dust with powdered sugar and serve!
