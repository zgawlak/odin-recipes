Honey Ginger Chicken

back to recipe list

Description

This vibrant dish is defined by its succulent chicken pieces coated in a glaze that harmonizes the natural sweetness of honey with the distinctive zing of ginger. A symphony of spices and aromatics perfectly complements the tender chicken, infusing it with layers of tantalizing flavors.

This recipe takes a mere 20 minutes! Serve with white rice and your choice of vegetable for a filling, delicious supper.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces chicken thigh, boneless,
    skin-on
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch

Honey Ginger Sauce

  • 3 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 tablespoon sake (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, honey, ginger, garlic, and sake to prepare the sauce.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper, then coat the chicken with a thin layer of cornstarch on both sides.
  3. In a pan, drizzle in oil, and turn the heat to medium-high. Once the pan is hot, add teh chicken, skin side facing down.
  4. Pan-fry the chicken for 4 minutes on each side or until crispy and golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  5. Pour in the sauce, mix, and simmer for 30 seconds.
  6. Add the chicken back in and cook for 1 minute on each side on high heat.
