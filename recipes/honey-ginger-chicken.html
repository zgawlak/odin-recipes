Honey Ginger Chicken

Description

Sweet and savoury chicken thighs. A simple, quick, and delicious dinner.

Juicy chicken and crispy skin, this recipe can takes a mere 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces chicken thigh, boneless, skin-on
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch

Honey Ginger Sauce

  • 3 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 tablespoon sake (optional)
